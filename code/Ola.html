Ola Cabs (ANI Tech. Pvt. Ltd.)

Product Design at Ola


I joined Ola Design team as an Product design Intern in summer 2019 for 3 months. During this course of time I worked closely with driver design, Product and Bussiness team. This was a great opportunity for me as, I got quite a good exposure of how a company manages customer as well as partner platform.

As the leading ride-hailing service in India, Ola employs over 800,000 driver-partners across the country. Ola is always on-boarding new driver-partners across tier 1, 2 and 3 cities to keep up with the ever-increasing, at-times seasonal, demand. So, they have to maintain this strength of their driver-partner for longer duration to give good service to their customer base.

Introduction
During the course of my internship I worked closely with Product managers, Business and tech people. I had a good exposure to how a company works and how it delivers products. Ola product has 2 verticals - Driver-partner side and Consumer side. I was a part of driver side mentored by Deepak Jose and Kavya.
Driver side system and users were bit complex so it took almost a week to me to understand the system. Soon after I got an overview I started working on ongoing project - Redesign of Accounting section of driver app with Kavya. I joined her on field research with drivers and attended various meetings with product managers. After some time I got a better understanding of system and I started working on Redesign of Accounting Section of Operator app and later on Incentive Model for drivers.
