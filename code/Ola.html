Incentive model for Partners

Product Design at Ola


I joined Ola Design team as an Product design Intern in summer 2019 for 3 months. During this course of time I worked closely with driver design, Product and Bussiness team. Ola is always on-boarding new driver-partners across tier 1, 2 and 3 cities to keep up with the ever-increasing, at-times seasonal, demand.
